Cheese on Toast

List of ingredients :

  • Cheese
  • Bread

… and this is how you work the magic.

  1. Toast slice of bread.
  2. Place cheese on top.
  3. Grill until cheese begins to brown.
  4. Enjoy.

Pot Noodle

List of ingredients :

  • Pot Noodle
  • Water

… and this is how you work the magic.

  1. Boil some water.
  2. Peel back the foil lid and remove the sauce sachet.
  3. Pour on boiling water.
  4. Wait for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir.
  6. Wait another 2 minutes.
  7. Open the sauce sachet and stir into the noodles.
  8. Erm… Enjoy.

Spaghetti Bolognese

List of ingredients :

  • Can of Spaghetti Bolognese
  • Erm… that's it

… and this is how you work the magic.

  1. Open the can.
  2. Pour into a bowl (you may need a spoon to get everything out, especially in cold weather).
  3. Microwave it for 2-3 minutes.
  4. When it starts to spit sauce all over walls of the the microwave, take it out and clean up the mess.
  5. Put a plate or something over the top and regret that you didn't think of doing that from the start.
  6. Stir.
  7. Microwave it for another minute or two until it is hot enough to burn the skin off the roof of your mouth.
  8. Try it, scream in pain and toss it into the bin in a fit of anger.
  9. Go out and get a pizza.
